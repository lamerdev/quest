Stage 0: Our Solid Core

We’ve established the foundation of GemRaiders: basic dungeons, combat, XP, referrals, and stamina. The Alpha is playable — our crypt’s gates are open, awaiting fearless adventurers. From here, we expand to grander horizons.

Stage 1: Forging the Future

Next, we’ll refine our architecture, creating a clean, powerful core that supports massive adventures. Think of it as reforging your sword — sturdier, sharper, and ready for brutal battles to come.

Stage 2: True Party Power

Nobody likes a ghost teammate stuck AFK. Our new party and lobby system will show you who’s in, who’s ready, and who’s lost in the darkness. Form squads and march into the crypt with confidence.

Stage 3: Unified Alerts & Better Stats

Tired of scattered messages? We’ll unite them under one Notification System, so every player gets crucial updates in real-time. Stats screens will also evolve into mesmerizing leaderboards that highlight your might in the gloom.

Stage 4: AI Flavor (Economy Mode)

Enter the whispers of AI. We’ll integrate short, haunting text from ChatGPT to give your dungeons, bosses, and items an otherworldly presence. Smartly controlled to keep your curiosity — and our costs — balanced.

Stage 5: The World Boss Awakens

Gather your allies! A colossus of darkness will arise for the entire realm to battle simultaneously. The more players join, the mightier it becomes — and the sweeter the rewards for those who deliver the final blow.

Stage 6: Treasure, Gear & Loot

You want real spoils from your conquests? An Inventory System is coming, featuring gear, potions, and powerful relics. Slaying beasts will now drop tangible loot — ready to be wielded or traded in the depths of the crypt.

Stage 7: Full-Blown AI (NPC Dialogs & Dungeon Creation)

The ancient magic of ChatGPT grows stronger, enabling living NPC dialogs and procedurally generated dungeons. Every expedition can become a unique saga — shaped by the dark muse of AI.

Stage 8: Our Expanding Ecosystem

We’ll open GemRaiders’ core to the world, allowing others to forge their own Telegram adventures. Multiple “Caverns” can flourish, all bound by the dark fantasy universe we share.

Stage 9+: Guilds, PvP & Solana Token

Beyond, we dream of epic guilds, fierce PvP arenas, and unstoppable global events. Most importantly, once our community grows mighty, we’ll unleash our very own Solana-based token to power the GemRaiders world. Prepare for expansions that redefine the crypt forever.
